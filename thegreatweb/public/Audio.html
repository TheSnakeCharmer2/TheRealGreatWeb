Audio in HTML

Here is an example of an Audio player, with a short snip of a song:

Below is the code for a simple audio player.

<audio controls>
<source src="./Audio/ConscienceFadingsnippet.ogg" type="audio/ogg">
<source src="./Audio/ConscienceFadingsnip.mp3" type="audio/mp3">
</audio>

Audio players can have different looks and more options when coded with Javascript as well. There can be options to fast-forward, rewind, and more.
